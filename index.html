Learn More About Patrick's Pub

AquaMania is a three day music festival held on Lake Hopatcong NJ. This lake-life summer kick-off festival features great live music and good food & drink. More importantly, the event also provides an opportunity for charitable minded bands, businesses, individuals and attendees to help give special needs kids a camping experience of a lifetime.

As always, 100% of the profits from AquaMania will be donated to the “Elks Camp Moore for Children with Special Needs” via Patrick’s Pub’s 2019 Leap in the Lake Team (which finished #1 in Fundraising for the 2018 leap). The primary goal of Camp Moore is to promote independence and greater self-confidence in special needs kids by providing them with recreational and socialization activities in a relaxed and accepting atmosphere.

The AquaMania Music Festival is held every June at Patrick's Pub in Lake Hopatcong, NJ. Click here for directions, or follow us on Facebook.
